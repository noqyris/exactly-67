Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Exactly 67 ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy completely.

What we collect

Nothing. The app collects no personal data, sends nothing off your device, and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. There are no accounts and no sign-in.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your level progress (stars earned and your best solution per level) and your sound/haptics preferences locally on your device only, using the operating system's standard storage. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Network

The app is fully offline and makes no network connections.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages, collects no data, and is safe for children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new date.
