Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Exactly 67 ("the app") is a game that respects your privacy. There are no accounts and no sign-in. This policy explains the limited data involved in showing ads.

Advertising

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process information such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, and ad-interaction data. On iOS, the app asks for your permission before any tracking that uses the advertising identifier (App Tracking Transparency); if you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy, and you can read how Google uses this data.

Remove Ads purchase

You can optionally buy a one-time "Remove Ads" upgrade. Purchases are processed by the app store you installed from (Apple App Store or Google Play); the app never sees or stores your payment details. Buying it removes the banner and full-screen ads.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your level progress (stars earned and your best solution per level), your sound/haptics preferences, and whether you removed ads locally on your device only, using the operating system's standard storage. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Network

The app connects to the network only to load ads and, if you choose to buy it, to process the Remove Ads purchase. The gameplay itself works offline.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Because it shows third-party ads, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new date.
